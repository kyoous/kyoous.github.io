Reflection on WEB222

WEB222 to me was a really great experience with professor Navreet. I came into this course with absolutely zero knowledge of anything web design based, and came out of it with a pretty solid understanding of the basics thanks to his teaching. From built in javascript function explanations near the beginning of the course, to explaining more complex topics such as objects and dom, and validation using javascript, each topic was clear. However if not, the available resources from web222.ca and the internet helped me further understand difficult topics.

In my opinion, the concepts of web222 will most likely help me in actually creating a web portfolio for myself in the future. Also, the basic knowledge of web design will aid my in the further courses at Seneca and allow me a better understanding of how web pages actually work when I am browsing the internet day to day.

Overall, the one thing that I think that I learned the most in this course was the concept of integrating the use of different styles of code (CSS, HTML, JAVASCRIPT etc) to create a simple, beautiful webpage. I believe this skill will translate to the use of coding with c, c++ or even future languages that I hope to learn such as python etc.

Thank you professor for the great semester!
